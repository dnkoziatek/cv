I am an electrical engineer by degree, controls engineer by experience, and an environmental enthusiast by drive. My passion is integrating environmentally friendly practices into communities to help create a better future! I use my love of research, data, and project planning to design economically feasible community projects. My focuses are recycling, composting, energy efficient housing, water sustainability, and transport.
